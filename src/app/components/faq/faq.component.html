FAQ

Customer Service

Q. Do you have Spanish-speaking representatives?

Yes. For a Spanish-speaking representative simply call our toll free number 1-866-201-383.

Q. Do you offer any kind of medical counseling?

Yes. We offer free prescription counseling and Medical Therapy Management. Ask our staff about our onsite specialists for diabetes and non-narcotic pain solutions.

Q. Can I transfer my prescriptions from my current pharmacy to Premier Health Pharmacy?

Yes. Fill out a written request form, call or walk-in and our staff will handle your prescription transfer request. We can be reached at 1-718-661-0872 during business hours or toll free at 1-866-201-383.

Q. How do I know when my prescriptions are ready for pick-up?

We offer text and email alert prescription reminders for all of our customers.

Coverage

Q. How can I pay for my prescriptions?

We accept cash, credit and check payments for prescriptions.

Q. What is your insurance coverage policy?

We do accept insurance. Our staff will cross check your diagnosis with your health insurance coverage provided by your policy provider. To ensure a more efficient process, always have your insurance information and doctor’s contact information on hand.

Pickup/Delivery

Q. How long does prescription delivery take?

If we have received all the proper prescription and insurance information, your delivery should arrive in between 3-5 business days.

Q. Do you only ship to home addresses?

No. If you would like your prescription sent to somewhere other than your provided home address, contact our pharmacy team to make a request.
